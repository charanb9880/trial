Our Story

About Spice Garden

Bringing Authentic Indian Flavors Since 1998

At Spice Garden, we take pride in serving authentic Indian cuisine that celebrates the rich culinary heritage of India. Our journey began over two decades ago with a passion for sharing traditional recipes passed down through generations.

Each dish is prepared using fresh ingredients, aromatic spices, and traditional cooking techniques to ensure an authentic dining experience. Our chefs, trained in various regional cuisines of India, bring their expertise to create a menu that showcases the diversity of Indian gastronomy.

Our Legacy

Our Flavorful Voyage

Explore the vibrant history of Legacy Spice and how we've preserved our ancestral traditions while evolving through the decades.

The Origin

Our story started with a modest family eatery founded by Chef Anil Sharma, offering authentic Indian dishes crafted from cherished family recipes handed down through generations.

1998

Initial Acclaim

Legacy Spice earned its first "Top Indian Eatery" award. Our signature butter chicken and tandoori dishes gained fame, drawing food lovers eager for authentic Indian tastes.

2005

Growth

We relocated to a larger venue and broadened our menu to feature regional Indian dishes, with expert chefs contributing their time-honored expertise.

2012

Cultural Engagement

We introduced our cultural legacy program, organizing annual food festivals and culinary workshops to share the deep-rooted traditions of Indian cuisine with our community.

2018

Present Day

Today, Legacy Spice is a cherished culinary destination, delivering authentic Indian cuisine while blending modern techniques, all while staying true to our storied heritage.

2025
